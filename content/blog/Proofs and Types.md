## Single Turnstile vs Double turnstile
Double Turnstile is closely related to the valuations, while Single turnstile is related to a proof system for a particular logic system.

## Natural Deduction
A Natural Deduction system is a formalization of logic in which no formulas are axiomatically assumed as valid, but there are only rules of inference. To compensate for the lack of axioms, it is permitted to introduce any formula as a hypothesis at any stage.
