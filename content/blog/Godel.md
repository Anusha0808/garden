# Robinsons arithmetic : Logic Q 
Intended interpretation : arithmetic over natural numbers
# Representability in Theory Q 
- All computable functions can be represented in first order logics with nothing more than addition and multiplication. (Robinson's arithmetic)
- FOL could capture whether or not Turing Machines would halt.
- Quantifiers buy you a lot!

# Godel's First Incompleteness Theorem
There is no consistent, complete, axiomatisable extension of Q.
Corollary: arithmetic is not axiomatisable.
▶ You can pick any two of consistent, complete and axiomatisable


peano arithmetic : axiomatisable and consistent, incomplete
