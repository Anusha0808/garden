## INGREDIENTS 
- [ ]  1 cup + 1 tablespoon rolled oats
- [ ] 2 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
- [ ] 1/4 cup protein powder
- [ ] 3 small eggs
- [ ] 1/3 cup yogurt 
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
- [ ] 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- [ ] 1 tablespoon coconut oil
## INSTRUCTIONS
- Blend the oats in a blender or food processor until it reaches a flour texture. Add in the rest of the ingredients and blend until smooth.
- Add some oil to a non-stick pan and place it over medium-high heat.
- Drop ¼ cup portions / $1$ ladle of the batter onto the pan. Cook the pancakes until the bubbles appear on the surface. Lift and flip the pancake and cook for another minute.
- Repeat the process until all the pancakes are cooked

## (my personal) VARIATIONS & SUGGESTIONS
- `honey`: I have not purchased maple syrup yet. So I drizzle a bit of honey on top of the pancake once its cooked and off the pan. Since, we are not adding any sweetener, adding a bit of honey does the job perfectly!
- `bananas`: I also add mashed bananas into the pancake batter while blending, It works as a sweetener and fixes the consistency if it is a bit runny,
- `honey and bananas:` You can take mashed bananas, mix it with a bit of honey, add this "paste" on the top of the cooked pancake right before eating! The softness and gooiness of the banana, and just a bit of stickiness of the honey makes the perfect topping for our healthy pancake! Tip: Do not mash the banana completely, it is good to keep it clumpy!
